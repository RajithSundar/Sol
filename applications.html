Applications of Solar Energy

Solar energy is used in various applications, from residential and commercial power generation to agriculture. Residential rooftops use photovoltaic (PV) panels to generate electricity for daily use.

In agriculture, solar energy powers water pumps and irrigation systems, helping farmers access reliable water sources in rural areas. Additionally, solar energy supports infrastructure projects such as street lighting and remote telecommunications.

As more industries adopt solar power, the versatility of this renewable resource will continue to support sustainable development across various sectors.
